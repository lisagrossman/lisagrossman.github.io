Astronomy writer
& journalist

Writing about the universe, the people who study it, and what it all means.

I'm the astronomy writer for Science News, covering the cosmos from planets to the edges of the observable universe.

Before joining Science News, I was news editor at New Scientist, where I ran the physical sciences section for three years. I hold a degree in astronomy from Cornell University and a graduate certificate in science writing from UC Santa Cruz.

My work has been recognized as a finalist for the AGU David Perlman Award for Excellence in Science Journalism.
